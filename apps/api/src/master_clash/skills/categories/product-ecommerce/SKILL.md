# Product & E-commerce Video Production

## When to Use
- Product demonstration videos
- E-commerce promotional content
- Unboxing videos
- Feature highlight videos

## Video Structure

### Standard Product Video (30-60s)
1. **Hook (0-3s)** - Attention-grabbing opening
2. **Problem/Need (3-8s)** - What pain point does this solve?
3. **Product Reveal (8-15s)** - Show the product beautifully
4. **Key Features (15-40s)** - 3-5 main benefits with visuals
5. **Social Proof (40-50s)** - Reviews, testimonials, or results
6. **CTA (50-60s)** - Clear call to action

### Visual Best Practices
- **Clean backgrounds** - White, gradient, or lifestyle settings
- **Hero shots** - Product at 3/4 angle, well-lit
- **Detail shots** - Close-ups of texture, materials, features
- **In-use shots** - Product being used in context
- **Comparison shots** - Before/after or vs. competitors

### Prompt Templates

**Hero Shot:**
```
[Product name] product photography, studio lighting,
clean white background, 3/4 angle, professional commercial,
high-end advertising style, 8k quality
```

**Lifestyle Shot:**
```
[Product name] in modern [kitchen/office/home] setting,
natural lighting, lifestyle photography, aspirational mood,
shallow depth of field, editorial style
```

### Pacing Guidelines
- Fast cuts for energy (1-2s per shot)
- Slow motion for premium feel
- Text overlays for key specs
- Music should match brand energy

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Too many features (focus on top 3)
- Poor lighting on product
- No clear CTA
- Video too long for platform
